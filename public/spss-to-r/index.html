  • SPSS to R

I spent the first ~8 years of my career exclusively using SPSS and recently began migrating to R. My brilliant friend (and R master) Danny once told me that the best way to learn R is by struggling through it, and, unfortunately, he’s right.

Still, some thoughtful scaffolding can go a long way. As the TA of our intro stat sequence, I began creating documents to help students (and myself!) either learn R, or transition from SPSS to R.
